The Alexandrian Society, caretakers of lost knowledge from antiquity's greatest civilizations, are the world's premier secret society of magical scholars.

Each decade, only the six most uniquely talented magicians are considered for initiation, promising a life of wealth, power, and prestige beyond imagination.

Meet the latest six: Libby Rhodes and Nico de Varona, who control every element of physicality; Reina Mori, who intuitively understands the language of life; Parisa Kamali, a telepath delving into the subconscious; Callum Nova, an empath who influences inner selves; and Tristan Caine, who sees through illusions to a new reality.

Recruited by the enigmatic Atlas Blakely, they have one year to qualify for initiation by contributing to various impossible subjects. Five will be initiated, one will be eliminated, and the six potential initiates will fight to prove themselves.
