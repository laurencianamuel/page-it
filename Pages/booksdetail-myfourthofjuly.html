Picnics! Singing! Fireworks! It's time to celebrate the best day of all—the Fourth of July!

Newbery Medalist Jerry Spinelli and award-winning illustrator Larry Day team up to honor America's birthday.

A responsible little boy, eager to do his part, wakes up ready to celebrate his favorite day. He has many tasks to complete—pies to bake, deviled eggs to fill—before enjoying the world's best picnic, face painting, and a band concert. Finally, after a long wait, he can relax and watch the fireworks.

This delightful book celebrates small-town America, full of nostalgia yet very much of the moment.
