A young woman uncovers a dark secret about her neighbor and his mysterious new wife, leading her to fight to keep herself—and the woman she loves—safe in this stunning queer reimagining of The Selkie Wife.

When a cry wakes Jean during a tempest, she discovers a young woman in labor, drenched in the freezing cold. As the village midwife, Jean is puzzled by the woman's origins and assumes she is Tobias's new wife, Muirin.

When Tobias arrives for Muirin, Jean's concerns deepen. Why was her pregnancy a secret? Why does Muirin's demeanor change around Tobias?

Despite knowing she should stay out of it, Jean's growing feelings for Muirin compel her to investigate. The harrowing answers she finds put them all in danger. Can Jean save Muirin and the baby, or will her actions cost lives?
